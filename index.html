How I work now

Fifteen years of judgment, paired with AI.

I use Claude — at Ryanair and on side projects like Vaulto — as a force multiplier, not autopilot. Experience is what tells you when the suggestion is wrong, when the architecture is off, when the “working” code will break in production.

01
Judgment first

Fifteen years of shipping iOS means I know what “good” looks like. AI drafts; I decide what survives the review.

02
Velocity, not vibes

Boilerplate, scaffolding, refactors, tests — handed off to Claude. The interesting decisions stay mine.

03
Quality gates stay

Every line still gets read, profiled, and questioned. AI doesn't get to skip code review just because it types fast.
