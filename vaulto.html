Vaulto

Privacy-first finance for iOS. CSV in, encrypted on-device, AI that never phones home.

Platform
iOS 26+
Stack
SwiftUI · SwiftData · CryptoKit
AI
Apple Intelligence · Local MLX model
Pricing

Revolut users wanted a real personal-finance app without handing their bank data to a cloud service. Vaulto reads Revolut CSVs via the iOS share sheet, parses them in memory, encrypts everything with AES-GCM, and stores it locally in SwiftData. An on-device AI layer answers natural-language questions about your spending, without making a single network request.
